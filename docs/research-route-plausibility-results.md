# Route Plausibility Research Results (April 2026)

Sources: BASK club patterns, Sea Trek/City Kayak/Blue Waters/Cal Adventures/101 Surf Sports,
Latitude 38, USCG incident data, boater reports.

## Key Findings

1. **Sausalito-Tiburon-Angel Island triangle** is THE core route for all vessel types
2. **SUP is launch-point + radius**, not point-to-point — model accordingly
3. **Ocean destinations** (HMB, Monterey, Santa Cruz, Bodega, etc.) are power-only and rare
4. **~20-30% of kayak/SUP long pairs are unrealistic** and should be pruned
5. **Top 5 routes to validate first**: sau-ang, sau-tib, mcc/fbg-local, hdb-local, brk-ang

## Route Disposition Summary

### PRUNE (remove activity tags or suppress):
- hmb/mry/pry/scz/bdb/tmb for kayak/SUP — ocean, too far
- rwc/oyp/cop for kayak/SUP from Central Bay — distance/exposure unrealistic
- stn — already disabled, no infrastructure

### HIGH-PRIORITY WAYPOINT VALIDATION (popularity 7-10):
1. sau ↔ ang (all types, popularity 9)
2. sau ↔ tib (all types, popularity 9-10)
3. mcc/fbg local SF (kayak/SUP, popularity 7-8)
4. brk/alm ↔ ang (sail/power, popularity 7-8)
5. Central ↔ clp (sail/power, popularity 7)

### MISSING DESTINATIONS TO CONSIDER:
- Emeryville Marina (common East Bay hop)
- Petaluma River turning basin (Latitude 38 favorite)
- Drakes Estero (Blue Waters kayak tours)

### SUP MODEL CHANGE:
SUP should be "launch point + local radius" not origin-destination pairs.
Most popular SUP spots: Sausalito/Richardson Bay, Crown Beach, South Beach, Tomales Bay, Loch Lomond.
