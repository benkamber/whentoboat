# Design Partners, Target Users, and Go-to-Market Strategy

See full research results in the conversation from April 10, 2026.

## Key Takeaways

### Tier 1 Partners (lock in first)
1. **Freedom Boat Club** (100K members, 640K trips/yr) — white-label conditions widget in booking flow
2. **Dockwa** (4,000 marinas, 400K boaters) — conditions widget on marina pages
3. **BoatUS** (740K members) — safety education co-branding
4. **ASA** (617K certified sailors) — skill-matched comfort scoring as member benefit

### Top 3 PMF Experiments
1. "Is It a Boat Day?" landing page + $500 Google Ads (1 week)
2. Co-branded SF Bay Paddling Guide with Sea Trek (2-3 weeks)
3. Freedom Boat Club member survey (3-4 weeks)

### Fundraising Path
- Pre-seed: $250-750K (Michigan Outdoor Innovation Fund, REI Path Ahead, marine angels)
- Seed: $1.5-2.5M (Republic crowdfunding, TheVentureCity, Raiven Capital)
- Series A: $5-12M (Left Lane Capital, Summit Partners, Bessemer)

### One-Line Pitch
"AllTrails for water — the free app that tells 100M recreational boaters whether today is a good day to go out, and where to go."
